Redesign concepts — app icon, home screen, spin mechanic

1. App icon concepts

Four tighter executions of the shield/U crest, tuned for legibility at small icon sizes (the current concept art reads great at poster size but loses detail at 40×40 home-screen scale). B is recommended as the app icon; A works better as an in-app wordmark lockup.

A · Refined crest
Same shield language, simplified bevel and thicker stroke weight for clarity at a distance.
Recommended
B · Monogram medallion
Circular badge, single silhouette. Reads instantly at app-icon scale, no fine detail to lose.
C · Angular plate
Hexagonal crest, more team-jersey feel. Good candidate for a "franchise" sub-brand later.
D · Flat store icon
Maximum-contrast fallback for App Store / favicon contexts where detail disappears entirely.

Replaces a blind random spin with a timing-based lock. Each reel sweeps on its own rhythm; tap LOCK to freeze it. Landing inside the gold zone doesn't change your team/era outcome — that stays fair and random — but it grants a small bonus (extra reroll or a one-time OVR boost). Try it below.
